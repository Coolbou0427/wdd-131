Overview

Purpose

This website is designed as a platform for submitting and sharing technology product reviews. Users can provide detailed insights, rate products, and share their experiences, much like movie review sites do for films but focused on tech products.

Audience

The intended audience includes technology enthusiasts, professional reviewers, and consumers seeking reliable opinions and ratings on various tech products.

Dynamic elements

The website will incorporate JavaScript-driven features such as interactive review submission forms, dynamic display of user reviews, filtering and sorting options, and real-time update of rating statistics.

Content

Home Page

This page will serve as an introduction to Tech Review Hub, displaying recent reviews, featured tech products, and top-rated submissions. It will provide an overview of the platform and invite users to explore further.

Submit Review Page

This page will include a form where users can submit their reviews for tech products. The form will request information such as the product name, a detailed review, and a rating. Additional fields may be included for multimedia content like images or videos.
